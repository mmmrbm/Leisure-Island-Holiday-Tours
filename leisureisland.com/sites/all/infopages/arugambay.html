Arugambay

One out of ten world's best beaches for surfing, the arugam bay, is located in Ampara District's fishing village of pothuvil in southeast coast of Sri Lanka. About 3km long beach is very popular in April to October because of the big waves it creates for surfing. Arugam Surf Point has a very long, consistent, section-like right hand break. Apart from the surfing, tourists can enjoy bird watching in near- by kumana bird sanctuary. Lahugala national park is about 30km from arugam bay where you can see many Asian elephants. Many Buddhist temples and archaeological sites can be seen around the arugam bay.
