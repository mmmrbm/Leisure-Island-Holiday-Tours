Bentota

A coastal town of Benthota is situated on the southern bank of the Bentota River mouth, about 65Km south from Colombo. Benthota River and lagoon provide one of a perfect places to canoeing, jet-skiing, boat trips for bird and reptile watching and fishing in Sri Lanka. Stunning Benthota beach is an ideal place for swimming, body-surfing, diving, jet-skiing, windsurfing, parasailing and relaxing. Apart from the sea and the river, other attractions in benthota and suburbs are turtle hatchery that located on Induruwa beach, a horticulture marvel of Brief Garden, Meetiyagoda Moonstone mine and Kande Viharaya in Aluthgama Which has the tallest sitting Buddha statue(160ft) in the world.
