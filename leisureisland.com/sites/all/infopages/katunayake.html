Katunayake

Having Sri Lanka's major international airport, the Bandaranaike International Airport, gives this city a noticeable place in the Sri Lankan map. Airport's duty free shops give a perfect place to shop items such as electrical & electronic household appliances, international brands of wines, whiskeys, perfumes, chocolates. Via 25.8km long Katunayake highway, one can reach Colombo within 15 minutes. Katunayake beach is another example for impressive beaches of the island, as visitors can swim, surf, wind sail and dive mainly months of April to September. Close proximity to Negombo city and negombo lagoon, a major tourist destination of Sri Lanka, add an extra advantage to Katunayake city.
