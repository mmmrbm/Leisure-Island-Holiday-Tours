Chilaw

Chilaw, also called Halawatha, is a fishing town situated in Puthalam District about 72 Km north to Colombo. Chilaw lagoon, which is a small tidal lagoon, is the biggest attraction of the city. Beautiful Chilaw beach is another place to visit which gives an opportunity to taste the famous chilaw seafood. Prawn farming has become quite popular in the Chilaw lagoon apart from fishing. There is a Hindu temple called Munneswaram which organize the annual chariot festival in the months of August and September.
