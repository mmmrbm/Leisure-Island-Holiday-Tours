Hambantota

Hambanthota is Situated in southeast of Sri Lanka about 120Km from Galle. Proximity to Sri Lanka's major national parks such as Yala National Park, Bundala National Park, Kalamatiya Bird Sanctuary, Madunagala Sanctuaries, Wirawila Tissa Sanctuary and an ancient Buddhist heritage of Situlpawwa cave complex temple make this city a base of major tourist attraction. Being placed in coastal and hot arid climate makes Hambanthota a perfect place for salt pans. British colonial era built Martello tower is another landmark of the city. Recently added Magampura Mahinda Rajapaksa Port, international cricket stadium and Mattala International Airport will be helpful to the development of this dynamic city.
