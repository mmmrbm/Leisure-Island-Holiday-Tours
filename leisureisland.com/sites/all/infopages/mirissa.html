Mirissa

Mirissa is a small coastal town in southern Sri Lanka about 240Km from Colombo. The Mirissa beach, yet another perfect example for a beautiful beach of Sri Lanka, is the biggest attraction as it is not only an ideal place for relaxation and also for surfing, snorkeling, boating, sailing. Ocean around mirissa is considered one of the best places for whale and dolphin watching in the island, the whale watching season starts from October to April. The whale species which can be seen are blue whale, bryde's whale, sperm whale, killer whale and pilot whale. Common dolphins, Bottlenose dolphins, Spinner dolphins, Risso's dolphins and Striped dolphins are the dolphins commonly spotted in the area.
