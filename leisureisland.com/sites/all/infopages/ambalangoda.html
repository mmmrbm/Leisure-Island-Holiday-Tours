Ambalangoda

This coastal town is situated in southern province about 107Km south to Colombo. Ambalangoda is famous for its ancient devil masks, masks are carved from Kaduru wood, and vibrant colorful devil dances. Ambalangoda Mask Museum is such a place where you can watch these masks and dancing and also you can buy genuine masks from here. Fishing Market and fishing harbor, Galgoda Sailatalaramaya Maha Vihara Temple are other attractions to point out. Sri Lankan most famous tourist destinations such as Madu Ganga Wetlands and Hikkaduwa are at closer proximity to Ambalangoda town.
