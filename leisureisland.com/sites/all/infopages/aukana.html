Avukana

Carved out of a large granite rock face with a total height of 42feet Aukana statue is the tallest Buddha in Sri Lanka located near Kala wewa reservoir in North Central province. It depicts Asisa mudra a variation of Abhaya mudra is a magnificent creation done during the reign of king Dhathusena in 5th century BC.
It is said that the degree of the alignment of this statue is such that raindrop on the nose would drop straight down to small depression carved between the toes.
